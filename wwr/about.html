Rafting Company Adventure

We are a group of young adventurers who decided to leave the offices to encourage extreme water sports. In recent years we have de-stressed more than 30 people per month through Rafting.

History

In 2020, Maria Bardales worked remotely in front of a computer and decided to make a big change by breaking routines. She rented a part of the mighty river and with the help of a specialist created her company in early 2021 that offers relief in times of stress "Rafting"
